### ğŸŒ Problem Statement

Rural India faces critical healthcare challenges:
- **Doctor Shortage**: Only 1 doctor per 10,000+ population in rural areas
- **Limited Diagnostics**: Lack of specialized diagnostic facilities
- **Access Barriers**: Long distances to nearest hospitals
- **Information Gap**: Limited awareness of government health schemes
- **Affordability**: High costs of specialized treatments

### ğŸ’¡ Our Solution

RuralCare addresses these challenges through:
- **AI-Powered Diagnostics**: Cancer detection, medicine identification
- **Telemedicine**: Video/audio consultations with verified doctors
- **Blockchain Security**: Immutable medical records and prescription verification
- **Offline Support**: Works in low-connectivity areas
- **Voice Assistant**: Multi-lingual AI medical guidance
- **AI Medical Chatbot**: 24/7 context-aware medical advice with access to patient records
- **Telegram Notifications**: Instant alerts and two-way communication
- **Scheme Discovery**: Easy access to government health schemes

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### About the AI Chatbot

**Q: Is my medical data safe with the AI chatbot?**  
A: Yes! The chatbot only accesses your encrypted medical records stored securely in our database. All conversations are encrypted, and the AI runs on Groq's secure infrastructure.

**Q: Can the AI chatbot replace my doctor?**  
A: No. The AI assistant provides information and insights based on your medical records but cannot replace professional medical advice. Always consult your doctor for medical decisions.

**Q: What languages does the chatbot support?**  
A: The chatbot supports English and major Indian languages. It can understand and respond in the language you prefer.

**Q: How accurate is the AI chatbot?**  
A: The chatbot uses Groq's llama-3.3-70b-versatile model and provides responses based on your actual medical records. However, for critical decisions, always consult a healthcare professional.

### About Telegram Integration

**Q: Do I need Telegram to use RuralCare?**  
A: No, Telegram is optional. It just provides an additional way to receive notifications.

**Q: Is my health data shared on Telegram?**  
A: Only notification summaries are sent via Telegram. Full medical records remain secure in the RuralCare platform.

**Q: Can I reply to notifications on Telegram?**  
A: Yes! You can reply to appointment reminders and interact with the healthcare team via Telegram.

### About Video Consultations

**Q: Can I start a video call with my doctor anytime?**  
A: You can initiate a call during your scheduled consultation time. The time restrictions have been removed for flexibility.

**Q: What if I have poor internet connection?**  
A: Switch to audio-only mode for better quality in low-bandwidth areas.

### About Medicine Identification

**Q: How accurate is the medicine identifier?**  
A: The AI analyzes images using OpenCV and Groq LLM, achieving high accuracy. However, always verify with a pharmacist or doctor.

**Q: Can I identify any type of medicine?**  
A: The system works best with tablets, capsules, syrups, and packaged medicines with visible text or logos.
